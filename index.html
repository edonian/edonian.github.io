about

Hello there, lovely viewer!

My name is Casilda King and I am currrently a student furthering my knowledge as a front-end web developer. I know a great deal of HTML and CSS, I can work with Wordpress and I have a solid understanding of Bootstrap. I enjoy designing simple yet stunning mobile responsive webpages and making them functional.

I am a proud nerd/geek who spends the majority of my time behind the brightness of my MacBook laptop screen. I live in Chicago but my ultimate goal is to live and work in the UK someday.
